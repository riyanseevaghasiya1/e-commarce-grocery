Introduction

Thank you for shopping with Grocery Go. We strive to ensure you are satisfied with every purchase you make. This Return Policy outlines the conditions under which items can be returned, replaced, or refunded. Please review this policy carefully before initiating a return request.

Return Eligibility

We accept returns for most items within 7 days of delivery, provided that:

  • The item is unused, unwashed, and in its original packaging.
  • The product is not damaged, altered, or tampered with in any way.
  • You have the original invoice or proof of purchase.

Non-returnable Items

Some categories of products are not eligible for return due to hygiene and safety concerns. Non-returnable items include:

  • Fresh fruits and vegetables
  • Dairy, frozen, and bakery products
  • Opened or partially used food items
  • Personal care and hygiene products

Return Process

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:

  • Go to your My Orders page and select the item you wish to return.
  • Click on Request Return and fill out the required details.
  • Our team will review your request within 24–48 hours.
  • Once approved, we’ll arrange for pickup or provide instructions for returning the product.

Please ensure the returned product is securely packed to prevent damage during transit.

Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you via email or SMS. Approved refunds will be processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method. In case of Cash on Delivery (COD) orders, the refund will be credited to your bank account or as store credit.

Please note: Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to a defective or wrong item delivered.
